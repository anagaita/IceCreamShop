About Us

Welcome to Magic MELTS, where every scoop is a little moment of happiness! We believe ice cream should be more than just a treat - it should be an experience that melts away your worries and brings out your inner child.

At Magic MELTS, we craft our ice cream in small batches using only the freshest, finest ingredients. From classic favorites to adventurous new flavors, every creation is made with love, creativity, and a sprinkle of magic.

Whether you’re here for a quick cone, a decadent sundae, or to discover your new favorite flavor, our friendly team is ready to make your day a little sweeter. So come on in, take a seat, and let life’s little troubles melt away — one scoop at a time.

Stay sweet, stay magical!

Locations

Coral Bay, Sweetoria Coast — Our seaside shop in Coral Bay is where sun-kissed days and ocean breezes meet scoops of pure joy. Locals and travelers flock here for cones after a swim or a sunset walk.

Pineberry Lane, Sugarvale City — Tucked into the heart of Sweetoria’s bustling capital, our Sugarvale shop is a cozy retreat for families, students, and anyone needing a sweet break from city life.

Lavender Square, Frostfield Village — Nestled in the quaint village of Frostfield, this charming shop is a beloved gathering spot where neighbors share stories over milkshakes and kids giggle with sticky, happy hands.
